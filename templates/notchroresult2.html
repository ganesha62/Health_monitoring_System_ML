Prediction Result: LOW chance of Chronic Heart Disease

HOWEVER, HERE ARE FEW STEPS TO PREVENT A CHRONIC HEART DISEASE:
  1. Eat a healthy diet:
    • Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
    • Avoid processed foods, saturated and trans fats, and excessive salt.
  2. Engage in regular physical activity:
    • Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise per week.
    • Incorporate strength training exercises twice a week.
  3. Maintain a healthy weight:
    • Monitor your calorie intake and balance it with physical activity.
    • Consult a healthcare professional for personalized guidance.
  4. Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption:
    • Avoid smoking or using tobacco products.
    • Drink alcohol in moderation or avoid it completely.
  5. Manage stress levels:
    • Practice relaxation techniques, such as deep breathing or meditation.
    • Engage in activities that you enjoy and help you unwind.
  6. Get regular check-ups and screenings:
    • Visit your healthcare provider for routine check-ups.
    • Undergo screenings for blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and diabetes.
  7. Manage chronic conditions:
    • If you have conditions like high blood pressure or diabetes, follow your treatment plan.
    • Take prescribed medications as directed by your healthcare provider.
  8. Get enough sleep:
    • Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night.
    • Establish a regular sleep schedule and create a conducive sleep environment.
