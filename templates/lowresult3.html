Prediction Result: LOW chance of Lung Cancer

HOWEVER, HERE ARE FEW STEPS TO PREVENT A LUNG CANCER:
  1. Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke:
    • Do not start smoking if you are a non-smoker.
    • Avoid exposure to secondhand smoke.
  2. Avoid exposure to harmful substances:
    • Avoid working in environments with asbestos, arsenic, or other carcinogens.
    • Use protective equipment and follow safety guidelines in potentially hazardous workplaces.
  3. Follow a healthy lifestyle:
    • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
    • Exercise regularly to maintain a healthy weight.
  4. Protect yourself from radon:
    • Test your home for radon, a naturally occurring radioactive gas.
    • If high levels of radon are detected, take steps to reduce exposure.
  5. Limit alcohol consumption:
    • Drink alcohol in moderation or avoid it completely.
    • For men, limit alcohol intake to 2 drinks per day; for women, limit to 1 drink per day.
  6. Stay updated with vaccinations:
    • Receive the appropriate vaccinations, such as the flu vaccine and the pneumonia vaccine.
    • Follow the recommended immunization schedule provided by your healthcare provider.
  7. Practice good indoor air quality:
    • Keep your home well-ventilated and free from pollutants.
    • Avoid using tobacco products indoors.
  8. Stay informed and get regular check-ups:
    • Stay updated with the latest information on lung cancer prevention.
    • Visit your healthcare provider for regular check-ups and screenings.
