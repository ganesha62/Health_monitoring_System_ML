DON'T WORRY! HERE ARE FEW STEPS YOU NEED TO FOLLOW TO MAINTAIN A HEALTHY LIFESTYLE
  1. Follow your doctor's advice:
    • Take prescribed medications as directed.
    • Follow any dietary restrictions or guidelines provided.
  2. Maintain a heart-healthy diet:
    • Avoid foods high in saturated and trans fats.
    • Limit salt intake to manage blood pressure.
    • Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  3. Stay physically active within your limits:
    • Engage in low-impact exercises recommended by your doctor.
    • Avoid strenuous activities that may strain your heart.
  4. Manage stress:
    • Practice relaxation techniques like deep breathing or meditation.
    • Engage in activities that help you relax and reduce stress.
  5. Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke:
    • Quit smoking if you currently smoke.
    • Avoid exposure to secondhand smoke.
  6. Monitor and manage your blood pressure:
    • Regularly check your blood pressure at home.
    • Take prescribed medications to control high blood pressure.
  7. Manage your cholesterol levels:
    • Follow a low-cholesterol diet.
    • Take prescribed medications to control high cholesterol.
  8. Control your blood sugar levels (if you have diabetes):
    • Follow your diabetes management plan.
    • Monitor your blood sugar levels regularly.
  9. Maintain a healthy weight:
    • Work with your healthcare provider to set weight loss or maintenance goals.
    • Follow a balanced diet and engage in regular physical activity.
  10. Attend regular check-ups and screenings:
    • Visit your healthcare provider as scheduled.
    • Undergo tests and screenings to monitor your heart health.
